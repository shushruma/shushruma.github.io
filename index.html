Professional Summary

I began my professional career as an Electrical Engineering student because of my interest in STEM as a high school student. After two years of EE classes and two co-op experiences, I realized that my interests and aspirations did not line up with a career in engineering. I loved the technical aspect of working with data and numbers, but my passion lies in understanding and working with people. So naturally I decided to continue my degree in Management Information Systems and Marketing. MIS appealed my technical side, allowing me to explore the data analytics, while marketing allows me to explore the business applications of human behavior.
